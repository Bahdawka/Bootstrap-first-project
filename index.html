Welcome to Bootstrap-Caffe

Café for IT Folks — a cozy spot with fast Wi-Fi, power outlets at every table, and coffee that helps you debug twice as fast. Geeky comfort in every byte!

Welcome to Café for IT Folks — where the coffee compiles without errors, and bugs retreat before a proper latte! What we offer:

  • Fast Wi-Fi: no more waiting for pages to load!
  • Power outlets: charge your devices while you sip.
  • Comfortable seating: perfect for coding marathons.
  • Real coffee, not just brew install;
  • A productive vibe: quiet, bright, and Wi-Fi more stable than production;
  • A menu inspired by dev culture: backend baguette, full-stack burger, and framework-fresh salads.

Drop by to code, study, or just reboot yourself. We love people who write code — and we do everything to make them feel right at home.

A café where the Wi-Fi is faster than SSDs and the coffee hits harder than your favorite JavaScript framework.
